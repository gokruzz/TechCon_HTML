Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the most influential tech conferences in the world. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees and showcased groundbreaking innovations.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, promoting diversity in tech, and connecting creators, thinkers, and builders from all over the world. Our mission is to inspire a new generation of problem-solvers and disruptors.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Moore

A pioneer in AI ethics, Dr. Moore spoke at TechCon 2018 on responsible machine learning. Her contributions have shaped modern AI policy frameworks.

James Lin

As the founder of DevNet.io, James discussed open-source scaling at TechCon 2021. His work has empowered global developer communities.

Priya Khatri

A cybersecurity leader, Priya presented a keynote in 2022 on digital privacy in the age of data surveillance.
